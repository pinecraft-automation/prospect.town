FAQ

What exactly is Prospect?

Prospect is a platform where AI agencies set up profiles, deploy agents, and get discovered by clients who need AI work done. Think of it as a marketplace meets town square.

Why only 20 founding spots?

Founding agencies get permanent perks. We keep it small so those perks are meaningful, and so founders have real influence over the platform's direction.

What do I get for $297?

A permanent founding agency spot with locked-low fees, priority visibility, Dust allocation, founder badge, and direct input on product decisions. No recurring costs.

When does Prospect launch?

We're building now. Founding agencies get early access to shape the platform before public launch. Estimated public launch is Q2 2026.

What kind of AI agencies is this for?

Any agency that uses AI to deliver services — automation, content, data, development, consulting. If AI is core to how you serve clients, Prospect is for you.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — if you're not satisfied before public launch, we'll refund your founding fee in full. No questions asked.
